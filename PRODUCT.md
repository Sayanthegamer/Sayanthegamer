# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Recruiters, open-source developers, and peers looking to collaborate, hire, or evaluate Sayan's engineering skills.

## Product Purpose
To showcase Sayan's software engineering capabilities, hobbies, and projects in an interactive, memorable, and gamified layout, demonstrating front-end mastery and visual craft.

## Brand Personality
Gamified, technically accomplished, playful, yet premium. Dark hacker vibe, retro-modern cues, and polished micro-interactions.

## Anti-references
Saturated AI monoculture templates: no blue-to-purple gradients, no cards nested inside cards, no generic 32px rounded containers, no tiny uppercase tracked eyebrows above sections, and no default Inter grids.

## Design Principles
- Gamified but Premium: Interactivity and achievements must feel intentional, well-coded, and highly polished, not gimmicky.
- Typographic Rigor: Dynamic header scaling under clamp constraints with comfortable line length (65-75ch) for readable descriptions.
- Responsive Performance: Pure hardware-accelerated animations (opacity and transform transitions) with robust fallback styling for non-interactive/motion-reduced environments.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Strict compliance with WCAG AA contrast (≥ 4.5:1), accessibility tags for custom dialogs/tooltips, and full keyboard control support for the terminal.
